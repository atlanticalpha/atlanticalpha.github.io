Atlantic Alpha

Independent Research & Investment Insights on Small & Micro Caps

About

Atlantic Alpha is an independent equity research initiative dedicated to the analysis of European small and micro-cap companies. We work with institutional investors, family offices and hedge funds seeking deep fundamental insights and differentiated ideas within less-covered segments of the market.

Investment Philosophy

Applying a fundamental, long-term and QARP-oriented investment philosophy, Atlantic Alpha focuses on identifying high-quality, underappreciated businesses trading at reasonable valuations. Over the years, we have built a solid track record in idea generation, deep-dive fundamental research, financial modelling and direct engagement with management teams.

Research Approach

Our research process combines a bottom-up, conviction-driven selection with a disciplined focus on business quality, capital allocation and alignment of interests. We seek companies with durable competitive advantages, sound balance sheets and management teams capable of creating long-term shareholder value.
